SpeekIO HR Management SaaS

Overview

Revamp and design a SaaS system in which recruitment process will be fast by designing and implementing intelligent test system which will help companies to optimize their hiring process.

The Problem

Previously they have an outdated and weak system which was not enough for hiring process. Their UX was not properly designed and needed major overhauling of the system.

Target users

HR and Online test users

My Role

My job was to design an optimize UX for new system in which I studied and analyzed their previous work and involoved all stakeholders to get maximum input for the real insight.

Team

Team worked in Agile environment included: 1 x Project Manager, 4 x Devs, 1 x UX, 1 x QA resource

Tools Used

Wireframes & Mockups with Adobe XD, markup with Adobe Dreamweaver + Saas pre-processor

Process Phase

Firstly, I created a general sitemap for my understanding of the system. Then I created lo-fi wireframes and sketches of the system and involved the client for their input and requirments. After validation from customer I moved to mockups and markup writing and handed over that markup to devs.

Final Outcome

The final shape of the product is ready to use by companies who are intrested to hire candidates with fast and optimized way by conducting intelligent online test and present real and rigid feedback to their customers.

InLogic School Management System integrated with Microsoft Teams

Overview

Design a system in markup language along with UX in which teachers and students can collaborate in real time and can exchange study material easily. The markup will integrate in MS-Teams in final stage.

The Problem

Previously they have an old system which was very complex in handling and using. Their UX was not properly designed with incomplete flows.

Target users

Teachers and Students

My Role

My job was to design an optimize UX for new system in which it will then integrate with MS-Teams as addon. I critically analyzed their previous work and worked on latest usability testing results with other UX resources resides at Denmark who gathered real data from teachers and students and then handed over to me.

Team

Team worked in Agile environment included: 1 x Project Manager, 2 x Devs, 1 x UX, 1 x QA resource

Tools Used

Simple paper sketch, Adobe XD, Adobe Dreamweaver, Saas pre-processor

Discovery Phase

After getting the usability test data from resource who worked closely with customer, then I studied the existing system and marked down stuff via gap analysis method. I created a list of missing and broken flows and what actually customer is looking for to make their system optimized. After that I proceeded lo-fi wireframes of the system in phases and took validations from customer on every critical step.

Process Phase

Firstly, I created a general sitemap for my understanding of the complex system. Then I created lo-fi wireframes and sketches of the system to understand the problem. After that I moved to personas and user journey so I could get the feel of the end user and usability. For that I did some R&D on this process of existing systems and references. After validation from customer I moved to mockups and markup writing and handed over that markup to devs.

Final Outcome

The final shape of the product was better in usage than existing system where users were unable to collaborate properly on doing assignments and homework and coordinate with teachers properly. The product was in good shape and serving users according to their need. Obviously it will get improve after getting post feedbacks on the system.
